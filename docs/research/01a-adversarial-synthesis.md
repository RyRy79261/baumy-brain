# POST-ADVERSARIAL SENTIMENT — memory-science baseline

Synthesis across the 8 topic ledgers (T1–T8) and the meta-review. Structured as durable core / weakened / broken / cruxes, honest read at the end.

---

## DURABLE CORE — survived the red team intact

These held under attack from both sides, mostly because the *data* (not the theory or the cross-field analogy) carries them.

- **Coarse functional plurality of memory.** The lesion double dissociations (H.M.: declarative destroyed / procedural intact; K.C.: episodic abolished / semantic preserved) survive under every camp, including the critics. "Memory is plural at the level of coarse functional dissociations" is uncontested (T1). Note the scope: this warrants *plurality*, not the specific four-box scheme (Dunn & Kirsner 1988; connectionist single-mechanism dissociations, Van Orden/Plaut).
- **The systems-vs-continuum question is genuinely live** (T1-C, survives/high). The Norris (2017) ↔ Cowan et al. (2019) exchange in *Psychological Bulletin*, Tulving's own SPI dependency, and Sherry & Schacter (1987) all check out. This is a durable *piece of contestedness* — the baseline is more rigorous for flagging it than for hiding it.
- **Accessibility ≠ availability** (T4-B, survives/high). Tulving & Pearlstone (1966): category-cued recall exceeds free recall for identical traces. A ~60-year replicated behavioral dissociation that directly refutes any pure scalar-strength or key→value-address account. The data does the work.
- **The Hopfield↔attention algebraic identity is real independent convergence** (T4-A core). Ramsauer et al. (ICLR 2021) is a genuine post-hoc discovery — attention was not derived from Hopfield — placing a content-addressable-completion primitive at the center of modern ML. This is the *one* convergence in the whole corpus that is not downstream borrowing.
- **Forgetting is lawful; spacing and testing are among psychology's most replicated effects** (T7-A, the only topic graded robust). Three converging meta-analyses: Cepeda et al. (2006, 317 experiments), Rowland (2014, g≈0.50), Adesope et al. (2017, g≈0.61). The *effects* are bedrock (the *curve form* is not — see below).
- **AI has no principled utility/time decay of stale-yet-uncontradicted facts** (T7-D, survives/high). Mature machinery exists for replacing *contradicted* facts (ROME/MEMIT/RAG supersession) and for *not* forgetting (EWC, replay), but there is no Cepeda-scale backbone showing a time-decay term improves downstream task performance. Temporal-QA benchmarks (TempLAMA, FreshQA, TimeQA, RealTimeQA) confirm models confidently emit stale facts; the field's answer is refresh/edit, not salience decay. Endorsed by both sides.
- **The parametric-editing ripple problem and cumulative collapse phenomenon** (T6-C, survives/high). RippleEdits (Cohen et al., TACL 2024) — locate-then-edit fails to propagate, an in-context baseline wins; cumulative forgetting replicates (Gupta et al., Findings of ACL 2024); AlphaEdit (Fang et al., ICLR 2025) provably preserves only *sampled* keys, so "delays not eliminates" is correct.
- **Supersession is logically unavoidable** (T6-A operational core). Any store representing belief change needs a read-time rule for what is currently in force; UPDATE/DELETE re-appear as logical operations regardless of physical storage. AGM (Alchourrón/Gärdenfors/Makinson 1985) and bitemporal transaction-time closure (Snodgrass & Ahn 1986; SQL:2011) are correctly cited.
- **Writable inference-time memory is a real, currently-undefended attack surface** (T8-C research-state). PoisonedRAG (Zou et al., USENIX Security 2025) drives attacker-chosen answers >90% with ~5 injected passages; MINJA (Dong et al. 2025) poisons persistent memory through ordinary no-privilege queries. That *no settled write-trust/provenance primitive exists* (only a 2025–26 detector arms race) is checkable and correct.
- **The frozen-weights / volatile-buffer architectural gate** (T8-A surviving core). At inference, weights cannot be written by experience and context cannot persist past the window; biology instantiates neither. This is a genuine, un-attacked disanalogy.
- **Importance has no dominant operationalization** (T3-D, survives/high). Verified by inspection of what real systems compute: LLM-rated poignancy (Park et al. 2023), access/decay frequency (MemoryBank), graph centrality (HippoRAG). Unlike recency and relevance, importance has no comparable normative anchor.

## WEAKENED / NEWLY CONTESTED

Still standing, but the escalated or "convergent" form is gone.

- **The master "convergence" narrative is downgraded to "shared ancestry + borrowing" almost everywhere.** This is the meta-review's #1 finding and it recurs eight times: ACT-R's declarative/procedural traces to Ryle (1949) via Anderson; Newell & Simon (1972) is itself a cognitive-psychology monograph; Atkinson-Shiffrin's store/buffer scheme *was* the von-Neumann computer metaphor; CoALA is an admitted import; HippoRAG deliberately imports the hippocampal frame; EWC (Kirkpatrick 2017) consciously imports mammalian synaptic consolidation (same Hassabis/McClelland lineage); Park et al. inherit recency+relevance from IR/recommender lineage, not ACT-R. "Independent triple-derived convergence" fails a genealogical test wherever it was asserted (T1-A, T2, T3-A, T4-A, T5). The *literal* "shared / borrowed and useful" claim survives without independence — but it can no longer be sold as cross-field corroboration.
- **"Episodic memory as category error" is definition-relative, not a fact** (T1-B). The sharpness ("precisely NOT episodic") depends on selecting Tulving's autonoetic criterion. Comparative cognition abandoned it: Clayton & Dickinson (1998) coined "episodic-*like*" with an operational what-where-when test *because* behavioral tasks can't reach autonoetic consciousness; Dunn (2004) argues Remember/Know reduces to a single signal-detection dimension even in humans. Under the operational criterion the human↔machine line is fuzzy, not a category error.
- **3-factor score ≈ ACT-R activation** (T3-A) survives only in a near-tautological form (every forgetting model is monotone-decreasing in time) and the seam is at *importance*: Generative-Agents importance is a use-*independent* one-time poignancy rating, which does not map onto ACT-R's use-history base-level frequency — internally contradicted by the same cluster's Claim D. Convergence, if any, is recency+relevance only.
- **Fan-normalization "same generative reason"** (T3-B) collapses three unrelated derivations: ACT-R −ln(fan) is Bayesian log-diagnosticity (optimal only inside a stipulated equiprobable partition), embedding hubness (Radovanović et al. 2010) is concentration-of-measure geometry, PPR normalization (HippoRAG) is a stochastic-matrix row-sum constraint. Same behavior, three causes — and not even the same function.
- **Need-probability "near-optimal"** (T3-C). The Anderson & Schooler (1991) correspondence is a match between two *aggregated* power laws, and Heathcote et al. (2000) show power-law shape is what averaging over heterogeneous individual curves manufactures. The three corpora (NYT, CHILDES, email) are one statistical family (heavy-tailed, Zipfian, human-generated text), not three independent confirmations.
- **CLS as "near-theorem / necessity"** (T5-1). It is a *sufficiency* result inflated to necessity: Ramasesh, Lewkowycz & Dyer (ICLR 2022) show forgetting *decreases* with scale as gradients orthogonalize — a single system buying pattern-separation-like protection with no second store. The defensible core is biology-agnostic ("benefits from separating fast sparse storage from slow overlapping integration").
- **The replay→gist AI bridge** (T5-2). RAPTOR writes summaries at *index/write* time — the opposite of the cluster's own read-time-emergence thesis; sleep-time compute is caching/amortization with vendor-self-reported magnitudes; the behavioral pillar (sleep-dependent gist / TMR) rests on small heterogeneous effects (g≈0.3) with replication trouble.
- **TEM ≈ attention as "the deepest cross-field result"** (T5-3). The derivation holds only for a purpose-built transformer *with hand-designed recurrent position encodings*, and its most-cited payoff (grid-cell emergence) is hyperparameter-fragile: Schaeffer/Khona/Fiete (NeurIPS 2022) show place-cell width 12cm yields grids but 11/13cm do not. A constructed relation narrated as a discovered equivalence.
- **The forgetting-curve parametric form** (T7-A sub-claim). Murre & Dros (2015) is n=1; Averell & Heathcote (2011) find an above-chance asymptote (decline-to-plateau, not decay-to-zero). The robust part is the *effects*, not the curve law — and SIMPLE *assumes* a log timeline while ACT-R *posits* power-law decay, so "convergence" among models that each bake in the time-warp is weak.
- **Forgetting-is-adaptive** (T7-C). A simulation (Schooler & Hertwig 2005, architecture-contingent; less-is-more has alternative accounts, Dougherty et al. 2008) + a review-article analogy (Richards & Frankland "transience = regularization," no measurement of an L1/L2-style penalty) + a pathway contested in adult humans. Courts Panglossian adaptationism (Gould–Lewontin).
- **Two-substrate "degradation laws"** (T8-A). The architectural gate survives; the vivid failure-laws do not. Spectral/rank collapse (Dong, Cordonnier & Loukas 2021) is a theorem for pure attention *without* residuals/MLPs — the paper's actual thesis is that those components *prevent* collapse; citing it as a deployed-substrate law inverts the source. "Lost in the Middle" (Liu et al. 2024) is substantially a calibratable positional bias (Yu et al., "Found in the Middle," ACL 2024).
- **Attack-surface "no biological analogue" absolutism** (T8-C). Shaw & Porter (2015) implanted rich false memories of committing a crime in ~70% of subjects — durable, retrieved-as-genuine, acted on with autobiographical authority. The honest disanalogy is *quantitative* (reliability, directness, ungatedness: a single verbatim `insert()` vs probabilistic reconstruction over repeated sessions), not categorical.
- **Encoding-retrieval as "literal implementation" in AI** (T2-C, T4-B). Hybrid/HyDE/multi-vector retrieval is engineered specifically to *break* encoding-retrieval symmetry, so the coupling is a defeasible default, not the Tulving–Thomson constraint instantiated. And Nairne (2002, "The myth of the encoding-retrieval match") — cue *diagnosticity*, not match, predicts retrieval — is a standing rebuttal the steelman never engages.

## BROKEN / SHOULD BE DOWNGRADED

Where the adversarial pass did real damage to the claim as stated.

- **"Append-only is fatal"** (T6-A) is broken — event-sourcing/CQRS is a live counterexample both sides accept. AGM cannot *ground* an engineering necessity: it presupposes logical closure/omniscience no real store, LLM, or human satisfies, and the recovery postulate the "typed operator" reading leans on is the most-contested postulate in the literature (Hansson/Makinson/Levi). "Standardized in SQL:2011" is committee provenance, not validation.
- **Over-merge "irreversibility"** (T2-B) is a provenance-discarding artifact. Append-only/bitemporal stores — *established as the field-convergent norm by T6-A* — make merges reconstructible, so it reduces to "irreversible if you build it to be." Worse, the biological parallel points the *wrong way*: brains over-merge (schema conflation) routinely and adaptively, violating the principle B recommends. Fellegi–Sunter licenses either cost direction. The meta-review's recalibration is right: this leans toward failing, not merely "genuinely-contested."
- **"Where is a fact stored is ill-posed → a disanalogy"** (T8-B) has its purpose inverted. Distributed, non-localized storage with read/lesion ≠ write/edit-locus dissociation is the *default expectation* from neuroscience (Lashley's engram search; modern distributed-ensemble work). If both substrates are distributed and both dissociate read-locus from edit-locus, B is plausibly a point of *convergence*, not a break. Hase et al. (2023) establish read-locus ≠ cheapest-edit-locus (an engineering fact about any redundant code), not that "storage doesn't carve a joint."
- **The spectral/norm-growth MECHANISM** (T6-C, T8-A) — as distinct from the *collapse phenomenon* — rests disproportionately on unverified 2025–26 preprints, and Rebuilding-ROME (Gupta et al., EMNLP 2024) showed a chunk of canonical ROME "collapse" was an *implementation artifact*. The phenomenon survives; the mechanism is preprint-stage.
- **"No write-time trust model in biology" / "no biological analogue" for errorful discrete insertion** (T2-D). The misinformation effect (Loftus), confabulation, and reconsolidation-with-error *are* discrete, propositional, durable false-fact insertions that propagate; source-monitoring (Johnson et al. 1993) is a real-but-unreliable write-time trust model. The honest claim is degree/engineering-maturity, not kind.
- **RIF as "biological echo of the ripple problem"** (T6-B) is directionally backwards (over-*suppression* of neighbors vs under-*propagation* to them) and reliability-contested (Murayama et al. 2014). And the misinformation-as-*overwrite* reading it depends on is contested at the root (McCloskey & Zaragoza 1985 modified-test → coexistence).
- **T4-D "survives/high" is over-graded** (meta-review recalibration). Its own crux — is coarse-to-fine hierarchical routing "completion" or "addressing"? — determines whether the *entire* unifying thesis ("associative completion, not lookup") is falsifiable at all. Unresolved falsifiability should not score as robustly surviving. The RAPTOR counterexample to "flat can't do global" is solid; the "hierarchy is the active ingredient" lean is one step past the evidence (no ablation holds hierarchy constant while toggling topology).

## SHARPENED CRUXES (the next pass should attack these)

Ranked by how many verdicts they move.

1. **Independent convergence vs. shared ancestry/borrowing.** The master crux under T1-A, T2, T3-A, T4-A, T5(EWC), T8-D. If the AI branch is downstream import, *every* "convergence" verdict loses corroborative force simultaneously. Residual test: would pure engineering pressure (bounded context vs external store; weights/policy vs retrievable knowledge) have produced the fast/slow and declarative/procedural cuts with zero contact with Tulving/Squire? That residue is the only thing that rescues any "convergence" reading.
2. **Misinformation/false-memory: trace-overwrite vs. coexistence.** One interpretation flips three ledgers at once. Overwrite → it is biology's durable-insertion analogue (weakens T2-D and T8-C's "no analogue"). Coexistence/source-monitoring (McCloskey & Zaragoza 1985) → it argues *for* retaining superseded state (T6-B). Currently adjudicated three incompatible ways, and none of the three ledgers cites the others.
3. **Does the need-probability / rational-analysis correspondence survive at the individual-curve level** (past Heathcote averaging + Zipfian same-family selection), and in non-Zipfian / non-human-text environments? This is the *sole* human strand T7-D concludes actually transfers to machines — so T7-D's "survives" silently depends on T3-C's contested anchor.
4. **Falsifiability of "associative completion, not lookup."** Does hetero-associative single-step attention (W_K≠W_V, one non-settling map — T4-A/T8-D) count as completion or a similarity-weighted soft lookup? Does coarse-to-fine summary-tree routing (T4-D) count as completion or addressing/indexing? If neither is excluded, the thesis is unfalsifiable.
5. **Over-merge irreversibility vs. provenance-preserving stores.** Is precision-first write a law or an artifact of provenance-discarding implementations — directly tensioned by T6-A's bitemporal norm. Does provenance dissolve the asymmetry entirely, or does silent under-merge remain the dominant hazard *precisely because it emits no error signal*?
6. **Do non-parametric memory stores escape the ripple problem, or merely relocate it to retrieval/reconciliation?** RippleEdits' own in-context winner suggests non-parametric stores may dodge the failure mode — but this is *asserted, not demonstrated* for cumulative multi-fact settings. Determines whether the entire parametric-editing collapse corpus (T6-C, T8-A, T8-B) is even on-topic for a supersede/reconcile store.
7. **Autonoetic vs. operational (what-where-when) criterion for "episodic."** Decides whether the human↔machine line is a category error (T1-B) or fuzzy — and, at the stricter operational reading (flexibility, integration, one-trial binding), whether current agent stores clear even the *behavioral* bar or reduce to a "queryable timestamped log."
8. **Write-time vs. read-time emergence of abstraction.** RAPTOR writes summaries at index time; MINERVA-2 (Hintzman 1984/86) has prototypes emerge at retrieval. This single locus decides whether the consolidation/reflection "same offline operation" bridge is mechanistic or a shared topology, and it recurs unlinked across T5-B, T5-C, T4-D, and T2.

**Calibration flags for the adjudicators** (from the meta-review, worth carrying forward): the misinformation effect is scored three incompatible ways across T2/T6/T8; the attention↔Hopfield move is weighed loosely in T4, defended in T8, dismissed (as TEM≈attention) in T5; and the Heathcote averaging artifact is fatal to T3-C's power law but waved through on T7-A's structurally identical curve-form sub-claim. The one-transferable-strand chain (T7-D depends on T3-C's contested anchor) and the ripple-relevance hinge (T6-C's open question governs T6-D and T8-B) were each re-derived locally instead of linked.

## Honest read — how much did the adversarial pass change the picture?

Less than a rout, more than a trim. The **empirical bedrock is essentially untouched**: coarse functional plurality, accessibility ≠ availability, the spacing/testing effects, the parametric-editing ripple problem, AI's genuinely missing decay term, and the writable-memory attack surface all survived direct attack because *data*, not analogy, carries them. What the red team broke was the **connective tissue** — the "independent, triply-derived convergence" narrative that made the baseline read like a triangulated cross-field law rather than a largely-borrowed vocabulary applied to a new substrate. That single demotion (convergence → shared ancestry + useful borrowing) is corroboratively deflating across six topics at once, though not fatal: a deliberately imported scheme can still be a fine design heuristic; it just cannot be sold as independent confirmation. Two claims actually flipped sign under scrutiny — the "where-is-it-stored" disanalogy (T8-B) looks more like a *convergence*, and the over-merge-irreversibility rule (T2-B) has biology pointing the *opposite* way — and several load-bearing anchors turned out softer than billed (spectral collapse inverts its own source paper, TEM≈attention needs a hand-built net, need-probability may be an averaging artifact, the forgetting *curve* is oversold relative to the forgetting *effects*). The net effect is a baseline that is **more honest, better-scoped, and more clearly falsifiable-or-not per claim**, with its factual core intact but its grand unifying rhetoric substantially deflated — and with a real residue of verdicts (T5-4, T6-B, T8-A/B, the whole spectral-mechanism story) still resting on contested interpretation or preprint-stage evidence that the next pass, not this one, will have to settle.
